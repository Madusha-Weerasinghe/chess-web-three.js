chess pieces

rook

The rook, renowned for its straight-line movement along ranks and files, commands power and versatility on the chessboard. Its ability to traverse the board unimpeded facilitates control of open files and swift maneuvers between flanks. Positioned strategically, rooks collaborate to dominate the board's center, launch assaults, and safeguard key positions. Astute players exploit the rook's commanding presence to seize control and secure victory.
