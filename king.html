King

The king, central to chess, is both sovereign and vulnerable. Restricted to one-step movements, it maneuvers cautiously, safeguarded by its entourage of pieces. As the game progresses, the king transitions from a defensive stronghold to an active participant, supporting attacks and aiding in endgame strategies. Ultimately, victory hinges on protecting and utilizing the king's position wisely, as skilled players navigate the intricate balance between defense and offense.
