Knight

The chess knight, with its unique L-shaped movement, is a strategic asset, adept in both offense and defense. Its ability to bypass obstacles renders it indispensable in controlling the board and aiding pawn advancement. As the game progresses, it becomes a tactical juggernaut, capable of ambushes and strategic control. Mastery of the knight involves leveraging its agility while guarding against weaknesses, ultimately securing victory through adept maneuvering.
